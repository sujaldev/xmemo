How to contribute?

You can contribute notes or to the source code, either way any contributions are highly appreciated.

By providing notes

You can easily upload notes by just sending a message in our telegram group, Xmemo (the bot) will read your message and upload it in our database, hiding all the hassle of updating the database from you, simple as that.

The bot is now ready (might experience some bugs). You can join our telegram group to upload notes.

By contributing to the source code

You can contribute to the source code on after reading the contribution guidelines inside the repository, xmemo is written with python (django) and hosted on heroku so you must know at least django to contribute.
