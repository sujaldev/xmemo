How to use xmemo?

First let's see what exactly is xmemo?

What is xmemo?

Xmemo allows you access to an organised database of notes and resources you might need specific to Xavierians.
For Computer Science Programs you can search for notes by date or by keywords in the question, and there you have an answer, possibly multiple answers uploaded by different people or even the same person. You can also see below every answer how many people though of that answer to be correct and how many thought it wasn't correct, so you are getting reliable answers.

Please note any type of notes other CS Programs is currently not supported, working on this feature.

How to search for notes?

You can search for notes through two methods:

By date Just go to the homepage and under search by date enter any valid date. Please note that the only supported format for dates is DD month_name YYYY where YYYY can be either 2020 or 2021 . Example of a date in given format is 25 march 2021 or 7 September 2020.
